🛍 Online Shopping Apps Guide

Amazon

Amazon is one of the most popular e-commerce platforms, offering millions of products with fast delivery, secure payments, and exciting deals.

  • Sign Up :Create an account with email/phone.
  • Search Products : Use the search bar or browse categories.
  • Check Details : Read descriptions, reviews, and ratings.
  • Add to Cart : Click “Add to Cart” or “Buy Now.”
  • Checkout: Enter shipping address and choose payment method.
  • Payment : Use UPI, cards, net banking, or COD.
  • Track Order : Check "My Orders" for updates.
  • Returns : Request a return or refund if needed.

    Awareness

    Social media awareness

    How to Use it Safely:
    1. Think Before You Post - Avoid sharing sensitive or personal information.
    2. Use Strong Passwords - Create unique passwords and enable two-factor authentication.
    3. Check Privacy Settings - Restrict who can see your posts and personal details.
    4. Avoid Suspicious Links - Do not click on unknown or unverified links.
    5. Verify Friend Requests - Accept requests only from people you know.
    6. Be Mindful of Cyberbullying - report and block abusive accounts.
    7. Stay Updated - Be aware of the latest scams and security threats.
    8. Limit Location Sharing - Avoid sharing your real-time location publicly.
    9. Use Secure Connections - Avoid using public Wi-Fi for logging in.
    10. Think Critically - Verify news and information before sharing.

    Banking scam awareness

    How to Use Banking application Safely:
    1. Beware of Phishing Emails, Calls & Messages
    2. Never Share OTP, CVV, or Bank Account Details
    3. Verify URLs Before Logging Into Net Banking
    4. Avoid Clicking on Suspicious Links or Attachments
    5. Use Strong Passwords & Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)
    6. Monitor Bank Statements for Unauthorized Transactions
    7. Do Not Respond to Fake Loan or Lottery Offers
    8. Use Official Bank Apps & Websites Only
    9. Report Fraud Immediately to Your Bank & Cyber Cell
    10. Educate Family & Friends About Banking Scams

    Store Government Documents Safely:
    1. Keep Secure – Store documents safely (locker, password-protected files).
    2. Use Strong Passwords – Protect digital files with complex passwords.
    3. Enable 2FA – Add extra security for online accounts.
    4. Avoid Sharing – Share only with trusted sources.
    5. Beware of Scams – Do not click suspicious links or emails.
    6. Backup Regularly – Store copies in cloud or external drives.
    7. Shred Unused Documents – Destroy old papers to prevent misuse.
    8. Monitor Statements – Check bank/financial records for fraud.
    9. Use Encryption – Protect sensitive files with encryption tools.
    10. Log Out from Shared Devices – Prevent unauthorized access.
